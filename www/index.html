Nasty Bubbly

This was quite a successful entry to Ludum Dare 21 for the 72H team JAM contest.

Controls

  • UP and DOWN Arrows: Move the fish well, up and down.
  • SPACEBAR: Speed boost.

The goal is to escape from the landing net to avoid being caught and consequently dying in excruciating suffering.

Use the boost to go faster and and pollute the bowl by doing so, avoiding obstacles such as bubbles and pillars to win the game. The fish food increases your boost bar.

Enjoy! And for the love of nature, save this goldfish's life ! It's only a matter of Karma.

Gonp

This is a local, pong-like game with options. Each time a player looses the ball he gets a bonus. Each bonus has a unique effect, eg: changes the bar size, inverts screen or position, splits all balls on field, clears the field ...

This is a prototype made with custom game engine.
